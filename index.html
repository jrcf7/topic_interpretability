Rater information
Instructions. Enter your rater ID exactly as provided by the study coordinator. Your personalised set of topic hierarchies will load automatically. Rate each hierarchy on three dimensions (1–7 scale). Session takes roughly 15–20 minutes.
What you are rating
CoverageDoes the set of topics account for all major themes? Are important areas missing?
GranularityIs the level of detail appropriate — neither too broad nor too fine-grained?
InterpretabilityAre labels and descriptions clear? Could you explain each topic to a colleague?
